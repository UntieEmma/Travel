Featured Destinations in Lesotho

Thaba-Bosiu

Discover the historical significance of Thaba-Bosiu, the sandstone plateau fortress that served as the stronghold of King Moshoeshoe I. Explore the ruins and learn about the strategies used to defend against invaders.

Explore Thaba-Bosiu

Sani Pass

Embark on an adventurous journey through the rugged terrain of Sani Pass, offering stunning views of the Drakensberg Mountains and connecting Lesotho with South Africa. Experience thrilling 4x4 drives and encounter diverse wildlife along the way.

Explore Sani Pass

Maletsunyane Falls

Experience the awe-inspiring beauty of Maletsunyane Falls, one of the highest single-drop waterfalls in Southern Africa, plunging into a scenic gorge below. Enjoy activities such as abseiling or hiking around the falls to fully appreciate their magnificence.

Explore Maletsunyane Falls

Sehlabathebe National Park

Immerse yourself in the untouched wilderness of Sehlabathebe National Park, known for its pristine landscapes and rich biodiversity. Explore rolling grasslands, unique rock formations, and discover endemic plant species found nowhere else on earth.

Explore Sehlabathebe

Katse Dam

Marvel at the engineering marvel that is Katse Dam, one of the largest dams in Africa. Take a guided tour to learn about its construction and the vital role it plays in providing water and electricity to both Lesotho and neighboring South Africa.

Explore Katse Dam
