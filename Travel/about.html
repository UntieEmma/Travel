Our Mission

At ExploreWorld, our mission is to inspire and empower travelers to explore the world, create unforgettable memories, and foster cultural understanding.

Our History

Established in 1966, ExploreLesotho has been dedicated to showcasing the beauty and cultural richness of Lesotho for over half a century. From its inception as a local tour operator, we have grown into a leading authority on travel in Lesotho, offering unparalleled experiences to visitors from around the globe.

Our Team

Our team comprises passionate individuals deeply connected to Lesotho's landscapes, culture, and heritage. From experienced guides to enthusiastic customer service representatives, each member is committed to ensuring that every traveler's journey with ExploreLesotho is filled with authenticity, adventure, and unforgettable moments.

Our Achievements

Over the years, ExploreLesotho has garnered recognition for its dedication to excellence in promoting Lesotho as a premier travel destination. Some of our notable achievements include:

  • Recipient of the Lesotho Tourism Excellence Award
  • Featured in National Geographic's Best of Lesotho
  • Rated as one of the Top Adventure Tour Operators by Adventure Travel Trade Association
