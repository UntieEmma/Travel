Featured Destinations in Lesotho

Thaba-Bosiu

Discover the historical significance of Thaba-Bosiu, the sandstone plateau fortress that served as the stronghold of King Moshoeshoe I.

Sani Pass

Embark on an adventurous journey through the rugged terrain of Sani Pass, offering stunning views of the Drakensberg Mountains and connecting Lesotho with South Africa.

Maletsunyane Falls

Experience the awe-inspiring beauty of Maletsunyane Falls, one of the highest single-drop waterfalls in Southern Africa, plunging into a scenic gorge below.
